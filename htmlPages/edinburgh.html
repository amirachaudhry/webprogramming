Welcome to Edinburgh

Edinburgh is not only the capital of Scotland; it is known for much more.

It has a medieval Old Town and a gorgeous New town with gardens and neoclassical buildings.

Looming over the whole of Edinburgh is Edinburgh Castle, one of the main tourism attractions

Edinburgh Restaurants

Over the last ten years, the city has transformed into a serious foodie hotspot, with some amazing restaurants and bars offering the best of contemporary Scottish cuisine and from much further afield.
